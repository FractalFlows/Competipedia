about

Emergence from Fractal Flows is an online exchange system that makes it easier for engineers, scientists, researchers and students to search, discover, collaborate, acquire and exchange engineering information, data and knowledge products. The exchange system integrates and links networks of knowledge repositories and tracks their evolution and emerge a value from the perspective of various communities.

Knowledge Products include: experimental data, experimental setup, numerical simulation data, files containing data, simulation code, theoretical solutions, models, models results, equations, mathematical formulations, publications, source code, licenses to simulation code, etc.

Knowledge Repositories include: GitHub, GitLab, Zenodo, university databases, open access scholarly publications repositories, personal data server, a shared Google Drive, Dropbox, Box, or OneDrive repositories, etc.
